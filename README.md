# TechMed
TechMed's primary goal is to design and develop an accessible, AI-powered symptom-checking application that helps patients better understand their health concerns and supports clinicians with structured, data-informed patient intake. The club bridges the gap between technology and healthcare by uniting students from computer science, health sciences, and related disciplines to build a tool that promotes early health awareness, informed decision-making, and timely access to appropriate care — all while upholding the highest standards of clinical safety and data privacy.
